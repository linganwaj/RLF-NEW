Children's Health

Uplifting the Foundation of Tomorrow's Rwanda

Early Intervention for
Lasting Productivity

Investing in children's health is not just a moral obligation; it is a fundamental pillar for Rwanda's future societal and economic productivity.

Our Children's Health initiative focuses on foundational physical and cognitive development, ensuring every child has the opportunity to grow smart and stay active. We bridge the gap for families who lack access to specialized pediatric screenings and nutritional support.

Flagship Initiatives

Grow Smart Nutrition

Combatting malnutrition through community education and fortified food support for low-income families.

Bright Eyes Screenings

Early detection of visual impairments and cognitive delays to ensure children succeed in school and life.

Active Kids, Healthy Kids

Promoting physical activity and sports to combat childhood obesity and foster collective responsibility for well-being.

Why We Invest in Kids

Foundational health in childhood is the strongest predictor of adult well-being. Our program targets:

  • Cognitive Development Milestones
  • Nutritional Security for Vulnerable Families
  • Preventive Pediatric Screenings
  • Fostering Healthy Habits from Day One
"A healthy child is the greatest asset of a developing nation."
