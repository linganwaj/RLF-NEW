Data-Driven Transformation

At Rucci Legacy Foundation, we believe that sustainable health equity can only be achieved when programs are grounded in rigorous data and evidence.

Our research wing works to collect and analyze community health data, helping us understand the evolving needs of the Rwandan population. By focusing on Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs), maternal outcomes, and healthcare access barriers, we ensure our initiatives are both relevant and impactful.

Research Priorities

NCD Prevalence

Mapping the burden of diabetes and hypertension in rural vs urban clusters.

Fertility Stigma

Analyzing social and psychological barriers to reproductive care.

Digital Health

Testing the effectiveness of digital literacy tools and health apps.

Health Equity

Measuring the impact of subsidized care on low-income family resilience.

Expert Insights
