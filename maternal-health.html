Women's Health

Healthy Her, Resilient Communities: Empowering Rwandan Women

  1. Home

Comprehensive Care for
Dignified Living

Ensuring the well-being of women directly impacts maternal outcomes, economic stability, and the health of entire families.

Our Women's Health program focuses on equitable access to reproductive care and screening services. We address the unique health challenges women face, from safe birth to the detection of reproductive cancers, ensuring that every woman has the opportunity to live with dignity.

Core Initiatives

"Healthy Her" Clinics

Mass access clinics providing reproductive health screenings, antenatal care, and cancer detection in underserved regions.

Safe Birth, Safe Future

A specialized initiative providing support for safe deliveries and postpartum care for underprivileged expectant mothers.

Our Commitment

We aim to reduce maternal mortality and improve reproductive outcomes through:

  • Community-based mobile clinics.
  • Subsidized screening and diagnostic tests.
  • Emotional and psychological support networks.
  • Advocacy for inclusive healthcare policies.

Invest in Women's Health

Your support directly funds life-saving screenings and safe delivery support for women in Rwanda.
