Our Genesis

Born from a
Legacy of Compassion

The Rucci Legacy Foundation (RLF) is a tribute to the life and values of Bishop Leo Rucibigango, whose legacy of empathy continues to guide our work.

Our story began with a personal journey of our founder, Jean Malic Kalima. Inspired by his mother’s resilience during her illness and deeply moved by the story of a woman facing the silent struggle of infertility, he realized that healthcare is not just about treatment—it's about dignity.

What started as a support group has evolved into a sanctuary for healthcare equity, dedicated to ensuring that no Rwandan family has to face health challenges alone or in shame.

Our Vision

A society where every individual has the opportunity to live in good health with dignity.

Our Mission

To uplift underserved communities by providing access to specialized health services, fostering health literacy, and advocating for sustainable well-being.
